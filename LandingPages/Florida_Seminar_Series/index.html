Program

About the Program

Katherina Rosqueta, founding executive director of the Center for High Impact Philanthropy, will present The XX Factor- How to Align Financial Investment to Improve the Lives of Women and Girls. Panelists will discuss the five key dimensions of women's lives that research show are critical to women flourishing: Health, Education, Economic Empowerment, Personal Safety and Legal Rights.

Moderator

Thom Melcher

Panelists

Greg Valliere

Chief Global Strategist, Horizon Investments

Greg is a veteran Washington watcher, with nearly 40 years' experience covering political and economic developments for investors. A frequent speaker at financial conferences, Greg is Chief Strategist at Horizon Investments and is a regular contributor to Barron's magazine.

During his career, Greg has worked for many non-partisan research firms. For several years he was director of research at the Charles Schwab Washington Research Group, and was the Chief Political Strategist at the Potomac Research Group. He specializes in coverage of economic policy, legislation, the Federal Reserve and -- of course -- politics.

Greg is a regular guest on CNN, Bloomberg TV and radio, CNBC, Fox TV, and is a frequent contributor to CBS radio news. In addition to his column for Barron's, Greg contributes frequently to The Wall Street Journal and The Washington Post.

Kenneth Trippe

Director of Private Investments, Glenmede

Ken will provide his perspective on the state of the private markets and will focus on the new regulations within the Opportunity Zone Funds program. Mr. Trippe is a principal on both the private equity and real estate investment teams where his duties include investment selection and portfolio management. Prior to joining Glenmede, Mr. Trippe spent over two decades with the Equitable Life Insurance Companies, a part of the AXA Financial group, ultimately rising to the position of vice president.

About Glenmede


Founded in 1956 by the Pew family to manage their charitable assets, Glenmede is among the nation's leading investment and wealth management firms. The company oversees $37 billion of investable assets under management for high-net-worth individuals, families, family offices, endowment, foundation and institutional clients. About BRAVA Investments

A permanently capitalized holding company for investors that want to create long-term economic growth while meaningfully addressing gender parity. BRAVA invests in high growth, innovative businesses with a proven impact on women in a multi-strategy investment approach throughout the entire capital structure. Our focus is on companies with proven science and technology with the potential for hyper-growth as well as on mispriced assets. We bring value to our investments with not only capital but also our expertise in creating and measuring impact on women globally. For further information, please visit www.bravainvestments.com.
